Looking for the perfect rug for your home? We can help!

We believe that there's a rug fit for every home. It's just a matter of finding the right one for yours, and that is why we offer free consultation in store. You can check out the different designs, feel the different materials, find a whole range of sizes... Whether you are looking to spice up an old home, a new home, a big home, or a small home, we have a rug for you. We offer measurements and installations, and we will work with you until you are completely satisfied.

Send us an email or call us to let us know how we can help. We look forward to bringing a bit of knotty into your home ;)
