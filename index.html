A bit about our knotty rugs ...

Who are we?

Knotty Rugs is a small business in Toronto that works directly with local rug producers around the world to help bring beautiful, hand-knotted rugs to your homes here in the city. While we value sustainability, our rugs are made with quality materials and traditional techniques that ensure them to last. With over 2500 rugs to choose from in store, there is one suited for every home. To help you find the right rug for yours, we provide free on-site consultation, measurements, and installations. We will work with you until you are completely satisfied, and if it's not for you, we'll come pick it up even after a month. Sound good? Let's find a knotty rug for you!

  • Our Rugs

  • How many knots are in a rug?

    Our rugs are measured by KPSI (knot per square inch) and most range from 81x100 KPSI to 91/190 KPSI. This means that there can be hundreds of thousands or even millions of knots in a rug! Incredible, right? Similar to the pixels on your computer screen, the higher the knot-count, the clearer and sharper the design.

  • Where do the rugs come from?

    The majority of our rugs are Persian rugs that come from different regions across Iran. Each region uses a different source of material and their unique designs. We also have some beautiful rugs from other countries such as Afghanistan and Pakistan.

  • How long does it take to make a rug?

    Hand-knotted rugs take a few months or up to 2 years to make. Of course, this depends on the size of the rug, the number of knots (or KPSI), and how many people are working on it. And they are made with traditional techniques and high quality materials that ensure them to last.
